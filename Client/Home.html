Tokyo

About the city

Tokyo (東京, Tōkyō) is Japan's capital and the world's most populous metropolis. It is also one of Japan's 47 prefectures, consisting of 23 central city wards and multiple cities, towns and villages west of the city center. The Izu and Ogasawara Islands are also part of Tokyo.

Madrid

About the city

Madrid is one of Europe's youngest and most modern capitals. It is a lively city known for its nightlife. During the day you can experience Spain's most advanced cultural life with fantastic opera, theater and dance performances. It will never be just you and the city.

Bangkok

About the city

Ingen annan stad på den här planeten matchar Bangkoks förmåga att vara en modern och myllrande megametropol, med snygga hotell, läckra takbarer, fantastisk mat, spektakulärt nattliv, bra shopping och intressanta sevärdheter – till låga priser. Lägg till vänliga människor.
